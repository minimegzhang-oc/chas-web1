ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Charlotte, a 12-year-old tennis player from Farum (Denmark). I started playing tennis when I was 5, and from the very first swing, I knew this was my passion. Every day on the court feels like an adventure.

I train 5 days a week at the local tennis academy and compete in regional and national junior tournaments. My goal is to earn a college scholarship and eventually compete at the professional level.

Off the court, I love spending time with my family, reading sports biographies, and cheering on my favorite player, Coco Gauff!
